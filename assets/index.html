Ekospati is a holistic solution that utilizes technology to monitor the condition of cities in real-time. The application not only provides up-to-date information on population, industry, pollution levels, and air quality, but it also provides city planning recommendations based on accurate data. For example, if a city faces a high risk of pollution due to industrial boom, EkoSpati can provide solutions such as arranging industries in more remote areas or increasing green areas.

The primary Ekospati application is an integrated solution designed to comprehensively address traffic-related issues by incorporating crucial features such as an overview of active vehicles and pollution percentages. It serves as a central hub where users can access essential information related to traffic problems within a city. This includes real-time updates on the number of active vehicles and the percentage of pollution in the environment. The application goes beyond mere data presentation; it offers insights into the dynamic aspects of urban mobility and pollution levels. By providing a holistic view of these factors, Ekospati empowers users to make informed decisions and take proactive measures in mitigating traffic challenges for a more sustainable and efficient urban environment.
